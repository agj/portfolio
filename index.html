The lake

video game

  • download
  • watch video

A brief, oneiric game of discovery, wherein the character is called to a lake far away by his dreams. For Windows; requires updated DirectX 9. Use the left and right keys to rotate, and press both simultaneously to advance.

Created in three days, using the game-making tool Construct. All elements comprised by the game are my own. It was awarded ninth place among the 36 entries of the TIGSource competition I made it for.
