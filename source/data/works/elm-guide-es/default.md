I translated the entire Elm programming instructional guide “An Introduction to Elm” from English into Spanish. The subject is the Elm programming language, and this digital book was written by its own creator as the main resource to teach the language to new users.

My main motive to undergo this translation was twofold. First off, I'm interested in the translation of technical works, and this seemed like a good challenge to polish that aspect of my translation abilities. But moreover, I myself am a user of Elm, and I found that the process of learning to program in it helped me develop my skills significantly. I'd be super happy if this translation became a reason for more Spanish speaking programmers all around to get familiar with functional programming, and in the process get better at their craft like I did.
